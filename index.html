About Me

I am a philosophy PhD candidate at KU Leuven (Belgium), where I explore Kantian philosophy, phenomenology and broader theories of subjectivity. Born in 1995 in Chengdu, Sichuan, I read philosophy at Peking University (B.A. & M.A., 2014–2021) before moving to Leuven. Alongside academic life, I translate philosophical texts and co‑found educational non‑profits serving rural China.

Timeline

  • 1995 — Born in Chengdu, Sichuan, China
  • 2014 – 2021 — B.A. & M.A. in Philosophy, Peking University
  • 2020 — Scholar, UK–US–China Summer Institute on Comparative Philosophy (Prof. Chris Fraser), researching Pre‑Qin Chinese language philosophy
  • 2022 – present — PhD, Institute of Philosophy, KU Leuven
  • 2016 – present — Co‑founder & Director, Linyin Initiative
